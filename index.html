About Us

Our History

Holy Ghost College, Owerri (HOGOSCO) is an all-boys Catholic missionary school located in Owerri, Imo State, Nigeria. Founded in 1949, the school has a rich history and continues to provide quality secondary education to students in Imo State and beyond.

The Class of 1998 of the Holy Ghost College Old Boys Association comprises individuals who graduated in 1998 or attended the school between 1993 and 1998. The Association was formally established in 2017 and officially incorporated in 2023.

Aims and Objectives

Social Development

To enhance, foster and advance the social development of the members of HGC Owerri, 98 set.

Welfare Promotion

To safeguard and promote the welfare of members of HGC Owerri, 98 set.

Brotherhood

To promote good brotherhood, mutual understanding and cooperation among members.

Educational Standard

To promote, uphold and upgrade the educational standard of our alma mater.

Development Projects

To seek funding to support and carry out developmental projects that would be beneficial to the members and our alma mater.
